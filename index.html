A little bit about us...

Axon Automotive is a privately owned family business specialising in Batteries and Tyres. The business was started in 2014 as a mobile battery service. In 2016, we realised the need for battery and tyre service in northern Gold Coast and Axon Automotive as a shop was established.



Since then we have continued to expand the business by adding wheel alignment, minor servicing, brakes, suspension, truck tyre service, trailer and car rental.

We strive to stock quality brands in all our products, offering value and satisfaction to our customers. Our range of stock continues to grow and if we don't have something we will try to source it.

We have a vast selection of new and second hand tyres to suit all budgets, vehicle makes and models - from Sedans to SUVs and 4x4s. We stock Federal, Maxtrek, Silverstone, Rydanz, Austone, Minerva and many more brands to choose from.

We are the retailers of highest quality batteries for cars, trucks, tractors, boats, caravans, jet skis, golf buggies, bikes, mowers and solar systems. We have in stock Axon, Austral, Enirgi, Ritar and Century brands.

Axon Green, our refurbished batteries are serviced, desulphated, charged,discharged,recharged and then tested/retested. All these are done using the latest pulse technology. Each battery comes with a 3-month warranty.

BRAKES

Brakes are a critical safety system of a vehicle and must be checked at every service. A typical brake service would include:
Check disc brake and or drum shoe wear levels
Replace front and rear pads/shoes if necessary
Inspect brake fluid levels and check braking system components
Road test vehicle to check that brakes are operating correctly

SUSPENSION

Today’s sophisticated vehicle stability control systems help you to safely drive on today’s sometimes dangerous roads. They play an important role in your vehicle’s ability to accelerate, travel and stop with safety and keep you in control by keeping your tyres in contact with the road at all times. Any time a tyre’s contact with the ground is broken or reduced, your ability to drive, steer and brake is severely compromised. Your car’s suspension system is essential to motoring safety. Shock absorbers keep your wheels in contact with the road. An ineffectual suspension systems means your tyres and brakes can’t do their jobs properly. Apart from Shock Absorber replacement, we can also replace bad joints, rod ends and other suspension parts.
